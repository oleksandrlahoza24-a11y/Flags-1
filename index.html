Welcome, Recruit!

Helldivers 2 is an action-packed squad shooter where you and your friends fight to protect Managed Democracy. As a Helldiver, you travel to different planets across the galaxy to spread freedom and destroy anything that threatens our way of life.

Your primary goal is to complete missions, collect samples, and extract safely back to your Super Destroyer.

Core Rules:

  • Never leave a squadmate behind.
  • Watch out for friendly fire!
  • Use your Stratagems wisely.
  • Do it for Liberty.

Threats to Democracy

The galaxy is filled with dangerous creatures that want to destroy Super Earth. You will face two main factions:

The Terminids

Giant insect-like aliens that swarm in massive numbers. Watch out for Chargers and Bile Titans!

The Automatons

Cold, calculating robots with heavy armor and laser weapons. They require precision and heavy explosives to defeat.

Both factions are constantly trying to take over planets. Check the Galactic Map on your ship to see where the front lines are.

Your Arsenal

Stratagems are powerful tools you can call down from your ship in orbit. They are the key to winning any battle.

Eagle Airstrike

Fast and reliable. Use this to clear out small groups of enemies or destroy nests.


Orbital Laser

A massive beam of energy that tracks and melts the biggest targets on the field.


Sentry Turrets

Automatic guns you can deploy to hold a position while you complete an objective.

Pro Tip:

Always bring a Support Weapon, like the Autocannon or the Quasar Cannon, to deal with armored enemies!
